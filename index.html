 HOW TO USE JOB SCOUT

    


      
STEP 1 — BACKEND SETUP (ONE TIME ONLY)

      
Job Scout uses a free backend server on Hugging Face Spaces to connect to the AI. Paste your Hugging Face Space URL into the Backend Config panel at the top, click Save URL, then click Test to confirm it is online. You only ever need to do this once — the URL saves automatically to your browser.


      
STEP 2 — CONFIGURE YOUR TARGETS

      
Customize your search using the panels on the page:

      
    
        
  • Target Companies — Companies you want the agent to specifically check. Defaults include Adobe, OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, Microsoft, NVIDIA, and Meta AI.
    • 
        
  • Skill Keywords — Skills you want matched against job descriptions.
    • 
        
  • Locations / Work Mode — Your acceptable work locations. Remote is always recommended.
    • 
        
  • Job Search Sources — Toggle which job boards and sites the agent searches.
    • 
        
  • Search Preferences — Filters like Disabled Veteran Friendly, Remote Only, No Relocation Required, and more.
    • 
      


      
STEP 3 — LAUNCH YOUR MISSION

      
Click Launch Daily Scout Mission. The agent searches the web in real time, typically taking 30–60 seconds. Each result is scored HOT, WARM, or COLD based on how well it matches your profile, with a plain-English explanation of why each role is a good fit.


      
STEP 4 — EXPORT YOUR BRIEFING

      
After results load, use the Export Briefing bar to save your leads as a TXT file, a Word .DOCX document with live hyperlinks, or open a print-ready PDF view. All exports include Apply, LinkedIn, and Indeed links for every opportunity.


      
SETTINGS — IMPORT / EXPORT CONFIG

      
All your settings save automatically in your browser. To copy your configuration to another device, use the Export Config button to copy a small code snippet, then paste it using Import Config on the other device.

ABOUT JOB SCOUT

      
Job Scout is an agentic AI-powered job search tool built by David Fliesen of Cibola Studios. Each day it deploys an intelligent search agent that scans company career pages, LinkedIn, Indeed, and major job boards for opportunities matching your unique hybrid skill profile.

      
Unlike generic job boards, Job Scout understands the intersection of Generative AI and Multimedia production — identifying roles where your full background is an asset, not just a keyword match. Leads are scored HOT, WARM, or COLD based on fit, with a plain-English explanation for each.

      
Built with FastAPI · Hugging Face Spaces · Anthropic Claude · GitHub Pages
