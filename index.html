 HOW TO USE JOB SCOUT

    


      
STEP 1 — BACKEND SETUP (ONE TIME ONLY)

      
Job Scout connects to an AI through a free backend server hosted on Hugging Face Spaces. Paste your Hugging Face Space URL into the Backend Config panel at the top of the page, click Save URL, then click Test to confirm it is online. You only need to do this once — the URL is saved automatically in your browser and remembered on every future visit.

      
After connecting the backend, upload your resume or career document in the Profile Setup panel. Job Scout will read it with AI and automatically populate your skill keywords, target companies, locations, and preferences. Your profile is also used to calculate a numeric match score (0–100) for every job lead, so you can instantly see which roles are worth pursuing.


      
STEP 2 — CONFIGURE YOUR SEARCH

      
Use the panels to tailor the search to your background and preferences before each mission:

      
    
        
  • Target Companies — Add specific employers you want the agent to check. You can add or remove companies at any time.
    • 
        
  • Skill Keywords — Add the skills, tools, and technologies you want matched against job descriptions.
    • 
        
  • Locations / Work Mode — Set your preferred work locations. Adding “Remote” ensures remote opportunities are always included.
    • 
        
  • Job Search Sources — Toggle which job boards and career sites the agent searches. You can also add your own sources using the + ADD button.
    • 
        
  • Search Preferences — Apply filters such as Remote Only, No Relocation Required, Veteran Friendly, and more.
    • 
      


      
STEP 3 — LAUNCH YOUR MISSION

      
Click Launch Daily Scout Mission. The agent searches the web in real time, which typically takes 30–60 seconds. Results are scored HOT, WARM, or COLD based on how closely each role matches your configured profile. Each card includes a plain-English explanation of why the role is a good fit, along with direct links to apply.


      
STEP 4 — EXPORT YOUR BRIEFING

      
Once results are loaded, use the Export Briefing bar to save your leads as a plain TXT file, a formatted Word .DOCX document with live hyperlinks, or a print-ready PDF. Every export includes Apply, LinkedIn, and Indeed links for each opportunity.


      
SETTINGS — IMPORT / EXPORT CONFIG

      
All settings are saved automatically in your browser and persist between visits. To transfer your configuration to another device, click Export Config to copy a small JSON snippet to your clipboard, then use Import Config on the other device to restore everything instantly.


    

  






  

    
 ABOUT

    


      
ABOUT JOB SCOUT

      
Job Scout is an agentic AI-powered job search tool that deploys an intelligent search agent each day to scan company career pages and major job boards for opportunities that match your configured profile.

      
Unlike browsing job boards manually, Job Scout works from your specific combination of skills, target companies, preferred locations, and search preferences — returning scored, ranked results with a plain-English explanation of why each role fits. Leads are rated HOT, WARM, or COLD so you can focus your time on the strongest opportunities first.

      
Results can be exported as a plain text file, a formatted Word document with live hyperlinks, or a print-ready PDF — making it easy to track, share, or archive your daily leads.

      
Built with FastAPI · Hugging Face Spaces · Anthropic Claude · GitHub Pages
